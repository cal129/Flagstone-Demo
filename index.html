Mental Health Tips

Stay Physically Active

Regular movement — even a short walk — boosts endorphins and reduces symptoms of depression and anxiety.

Get Enough Sleep

Aim for 7–9 hours of sleep per night. Good sleep supports mood stability, memory, and stress recovery.

Practice Daily Mindfulness

Take 5–10 minutes each day to focus on your breath or surroundings. Mindfulness helps reduce anxiety and improves emotional regulation.

Practice Gratitude

Write down 3 things you're grateful for each day. Gratitude helps shift focus from stress to positivity.

Limit Screen Time

Take breaks from phones and social media. Too much screen time can increase stress and reduce sleep quality.

Eat Balanced Meals

Nutrition affects mood. Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and protein to support brain health.
